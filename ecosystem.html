Decision Intelligence is the current commercial focus, but it is not the whole build. NexoviaNet is also developing adjacent projects, expanding both across product surfaces and deeper inside the systems that earn it.

Current commercial focus
Decision Intelligence
Systems for high-stakes operating decisions.

The current commercial branch under NexoviaNet. Focused on helping teams structure and pressure-test real operating decisions where pricing, inventory, allocation, planning, supply, timing, and service tradeoffs interact.

Connects multiple operating decisions into one decision model
Evaluates tradeoffs across demand, supply, timing, and service constraints
Supports pilot-based work around one live business situation at a time
Beta release soon
DailyBuild
A workflow tool for planning, execution, reminders, and progress.

A workflow tool built around day-level planning and follow-through. DailyBuild is designed to help users organize work, maintain momentum, and keep execution visible across tasks, projects, reminders, and routines.

Supports daily planning, reminders, and project tracking in one place
Built for execution clarity, not just task storage
Can later evolve into an adjacent workflow layer for broader systems
Parallel branch
NexoFormula
Formulation, inputs, routines, and personal self-care systems.

A parallel branch exploring products and systems around formulation logic, inputs, routines, and personal self-care. This direction is distinct from the current B2B operating focus, but remains part of the broader NexoviaNet ecosystem.

Explores formulation-driven products and recommendation logic
Focuses on personal systems around inputs, routines, and self-care decisions
Develops as a parallel consumer-oriented branch under the same umbrella
